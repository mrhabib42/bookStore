Biography

Alex Michaelides is a British-Cypriot author and screenwriter. Born in Cyprus in 1977, he studied English literature at Cambridge University and received an MA in screenwriting from the American Film Institute in Los Angeles.

His debut novel, The Silent Patient, was a Sunday Times and New York Times bestseller and has sold more than a million copies worldwide. It won the Goodreads Choice Award for Best Mystery & Thriller in 2019 and was optioned for film by Plan B Entertainment.

Before becoming a novelist, Michaelides wrote the film The Devil You Know (2013) starring Rosamund Pike and co-wrote The Con is On (2018) starring Uma Thurman and Tim Roth.

Genre Details

Mystery

Genre ID: G1003

Description

Mystery is a genre of literature whose stories focus on a puzzling crime, situation, or circumstance that needs to be solved. The genre often follows a detective or other investigator as they attempt to solve the mystery through clues and deduction.

Characteristics
  • A mysterious crime or puzzle to solve
  • Clues that the reader can interpret
  • A detective or amateur sleuth as protagonist
  • Suspense and tension throughout the story
  • A satisfying resolution that explains the mystery
